Format:
cse111 LabNO YourID YourFirstName MiddleName LastName

Example:
cse111 Lab01 09080605 Muhammad Hamza Hussain

Note that
there is no space between cse and 111
there is no space between lab and 01
except for cse111, everything else is separated by one single space
when lab number is smaller than 10, add 0 infront of the lab number. For example, we wrote 01 instead of 1. However do not add any space in front of  10, 11 ... etc
